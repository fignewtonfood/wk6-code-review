SPEC SHEET FOR WORD REPLACER

1) Print input with no changes
Input: A
Output: A

2) Replace one letter with another
Input: A
Output: B

3) Replace one word with another
Input: Anteater
Output: Bear

4) Replace one word in string with another word
Input: Anteater sleeps.
Output: Bear sleeps.

5) Replace one word with multiple words
Input: Anteater sleeps.
Output: Anteater snoozes and dreams.

6) Replace multiple words with one word
Input: Anteater sleeps.
Output: Wow!

7) Replace multiple words with multiple words
Input: Anteater sleeps.
Output: Bear grills.

8) Replace multiple instances
Input: anteater and anteater
Output: bear and bear
